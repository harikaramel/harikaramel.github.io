Hariprasad V M

I am a research scholar at the Centre for Technology Alternatives for Rural Areas (CTARA) at IIT Bombay. My research delves into pressing social and environmental challenges, with a focus on sanitation and marginalized communities. Through my study, "Informality, Marginality and Agency: A Study of Sanitation Work and Workers in Alleppey Town, India," I explore the harsh realities of sanitation workers' lives, marked by precarious employment and tied to marginalized socio-economic and caste identities.
